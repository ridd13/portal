# Das Portal — Boilerplate (EN)

## What is Das Portal?
Das Portal (das-portal.online) is a free discovery platform for holistic and spiritual events in Northern Germany. We connect facilitators, coaches, and shamans with people seeking authentic experiences — from breathwork and cacao ceremonies to ecstatic dance and deep shadow work.

## Why now?
The market for "Conscious Events" is growing rapidly, but the digital infrastructure is lagging behind. Currently, most communication happens in cluttered Telegram groups or fleeting Instagram stories. This makes it difficult for providers to reach new people outside their existing "bubble" and nearly impossible for seekers to find high-quality offerings via search engines like Google.

## Our Approach: Zero-Effort & Maximum Visibility
Das Portal solves this problem through an innovative, automated approach:
- **Zero-Effort:** We scrape public event information from over 90 relevant channels. Providers don't have to do anything to be listed.
- **Google Visibility:** We translate "Telegram chaos" into SEO-optimized event pages. Anyone searching for "Breathwork Hamburg" finds us — and thus the providers.
- **Local Depth:** While other platforms try to cover the entire world, we focus on local density in Hamburg and Schleswig-Holstein.

## Key Facts
- **Launch:** April 2026
- **Region:** Focus on Hamburg & Schleswig-Holstein
- **Volume:** 571 upcoming events and 1,000+ provider profiles (as of April 26, 2026)
- **Model:** Free listing for all providers — no subscription, no fee.
